Creamy Irish Coffee

Recipe

* 3 cups hot strong brewed coffee

* 4 ounces Irish cream liqueur

* Sweetened whipped cream, optional

* Chocolate shavings, optional

Directions

* Divide coffee and liqueur among 4 mugss

* Stir

*If desired, top with whipped cream and chocolate shavings
